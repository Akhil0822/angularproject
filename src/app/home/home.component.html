A pizza store, also known as a pizzeria, is a restaurant that specializes in making, selling, and serving pizza. They may offer dine-in, take-out, or delivery services, and often include additional menu items like salads, appetizers, and side dishes. Pizza stores can range from small, family-run establishments to larger chains, with many offering a variety of pizza styles and toppings.
